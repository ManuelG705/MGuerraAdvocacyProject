Ocean Pollution

Pollution in our oceans is a serious issue that needs to be addressed. It's a major problem that affects the entire world.

Here are some of the major causes:

  • Plastic waste: A massive amount of plastic ends up in our oceans, harming marine life and ecosystems.
  • Oil spills: Accidents involving oil tankers and offshore drilling platforms can release large quantities of oil into the water, causing devastating damage.
  • Agricultural runoff: Fertilizers and pesticides used in agriculture can run off into waterways and ultimately reach the ocean, polluting the water and harming marine life.
  • Sewage and industrial waste: Untreated sewage and industrial waste often end up in rivers and oceans, contaminating the water with harmful bacteria and chemicals.
